A prayer , and also a thanksgiving unto God , for his great mercy , in giving , and pre - serving our Noble Queen Elizabeth , to live and reign over us , to his honour and glory , and our comfort in Christ Jesus , to be sung the xvii . day of November 1577. Made by I. Pit , minister . I exhort that supplications , prayers , and intercessions , and giving of thanks , be made for Kings , and for all that be in authority , that we may led a quiet and peaceable life in all godliness and honesty . 1. Tim. 2. Chap. 1 , 2 , verses . Sing this as the foure score and one Psalm . BE light , and glad , in God rejoice , which is our strength and aid , with joyeful , and most pleasant hearts , let it be forth now said : Thou art our Lord , thou art our King , thou art our only stay , to thee will we give laud and praise , and further let us say . Psal. 81.1 . Deutr. 6.4 . Esa. 33.22 . we praise thee God , we knowledge thee , the only Lord to be , for thy great mercy on us showed , as this day we may see : To thee we cry , and also gyve , most high thanks , laud and praise , for thy good gifts , which we receive , both now and all our days . 1. Chro. 29.13 Esay . 2.17 . psalm . 145.8.9 . 10. etc psal . 144.10 . O Holy , holy , holy Lord , shall be our daily song , for thy good gifts , bestowed on us , this ninetene years now long , And for our Queen Elizabeth , which so long time hath been , through thy good providence Oh Lord , our good & gracious Queen . psal . 99.9 Esay . 6.3 . Apoc. 4.8 . psal . 144.10 . psal 145.3.4 . The company of high and low , do praise thy holy name , both young and old , both rich & poor , with heart do even the same : Acknowledging , thy majesty , to be the only stay , through Christ our Lord & Saviour , our light , our truth , our way . psal . 148.11.12 psal . 145.14 . Ioh. 14.6 . The holy ghost our comforter , does teach us all in deed , how we should walk in thy true fear , and call on thee in need : For that our sins most grievous are , and do deserve thine ire , we pray thee pardon us each one , thy mercy we require . Ioh. 14.16 . 17. Ioh. 15. 26.27 . Ioh. 16. 8.9.10.11.12 . & 13. verses . dan . 9.5 . and 19. psal . 65.3 . And grant our Queen Elizabeth , with us long time to reign , this land to keep , full long in peace , and gospel to maineteine : In true obedience of the same , together we may live , with long life and most perfect joy , Oh Lord unto her give . 1. Timoth. 2. 1.2 . deut . 5.32.33 . psal . 61.6 . And give unto her council grace , through working of this sprite , in gospels lore , and common wealth , to have a great deligh The same to bring in perfect state , and so the same to stay , against all wicked perverse men , good Lord grant this we pray . 2. kin . 18.4 . 5.6 . and 7. 2. chr . 19.4 . 5.6.7 . and . 9. 2. mac . 4.4.5 . and . 6. Lord help thy servants which do cry , and call to thee for aid , that enemies thence be put to flight , and wicked men dismayed : And let us all , most joyfully , with hearts triumph and say , thy Name be blessed now , Oh Lord , for this most joyful day . Psalm . 109.26 . 27.28 . and 29. Daniel . 6.24 . Psalm . 97.12 . we magnify thee every one , and will do while we live , for thy great mercy showed on us , for this gift thou didst give : Elizabeth our noble Queen , which as this day took place , in Royal seat this Realm to guide , Lord bless and keep her grace . Psalm . 69.30 . Psalm . 34.3 . Psal. 103.1.2 . Judit . 15.9.10 Dani. 6.21 . From foreign foes , Oh Lord her keep , and enemies at home , from feigned friends , and traitorous hearts , preserve her Lord alone : For thou only art her defence , in thee does she whole trust , save and keep her , Oh Lord therefore , for thy mercies most just . 2. Kings . 19.19 . Judith . 13.4.5 . Ester . 7.3.5 . 6.9 . and 10. Psal. 18.47.48 . 49. and 50. Psal. 98.3 . O Lord our trust and confidence , we do repose in thee , for thou dost never fail them Lord , that do put trust in thee : With faithful hearts , we do now pray , that thou wilt so maintain , our gracious Queen Elizabeth , long over us to reign . Psal. 11.1 . Psalm . 10.10 . nehem . 2.3 . Daniel . 6.21 . Then shall we sing with joyful hearts , All glory be to thee , the Father , Son , and holy Ghost , which be in persons three : As it hath be in all the time , that hath be heretofore , as it is now , and so shall be , hence forth for evermore . Amen . Luk. 2.14 . mat . 28.19 . 1. Thessalonians the v. Chap. 16 , 17 , and 18 verses . Rejoice ever . Pray continually . In all things give thanks , for this is the will of God in Christ Jesus towards you . FINIS . Imprinted by Christopher Barkar . Aloud by authority .
